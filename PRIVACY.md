# Privacy Policy for YFull Finder (Ajwla)

**Last Updated:** May 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the **YFull Finder** browser extension ("we," "our," or "the extension") handles user data. Our primary goal is to provide a tool for genetic genealogy research while respecting your privacy.

## 1. Data We Collect

The extension is designed to be as "lean" as possible. We only collect data that is strictly necessary for the core functionality of the service:

*   **Authentication & Identity:** When you sign up or log in, we collect your **Email Address** and a **Username (Display Name)**. These are used to create your profile and attribute the DNA samples you contribute to the community database.
*   **Profile Avatars:** Users can optionally upload a **Profile Picture**. These images are compressed, converted to WebP format, and stored in project storage.
*   **Authentication Credentials:** Passwords and session tokens are handled by the authentication system and are used only for account access.
*   **Website Content:** To provide its core function, the extension reads **Sample IDs** (e.g., YF12345) from the `yfull.com` domain. This data is used only to query our database for matching genealogical information to display to you.
*   **Voluntary Genealogical Metadata:** Users may add surnames, ethnic groups, regions, source links, G25 coordinates, and ancestral or sample location coordinates as public community metadata.

## 2. Data Usage

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:
*   **Database Enrichment:** To display surnames, tribes, and ancestral regions next to samples on YFull.com.
*   **User Profiles:** To track your personal contribution statistics (how many samples you've added).
*   **Security:** To prevent spam and unauthorized access to our backend via authenticated sessions.
*   **YFull Overlay:** To show community metadata on top of YFull pages without modifying YFull scientific data.

**We DO NOT:**
*   Sell your data to third parties.
*   Store your browsing history outside of the `yfull.com` domain.
*   Use your data for marketing or advertising purposes.
*   Collect financial, medical, or current device location information.

Location fields entered by users refer to ancestral or sample-related places and are treated as genealogical metadata, not as the user's live location.

## 3. Data Storage & Security

*   **Device Data:** The extension stores only the local data needed for account access and user preferences.
*   **Project Database:** Your profile data and contributed samples are stored in the project database.
*   **Encryption:** All communication between the extension and our backend is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

## 4. User Control & Privacy Choices

*   **Anonymous Contributions:** Users can choose to hide public attribution when adding samples. The genealogical data remains part of the community database.
*   **Leaderboard Privacy:** You can opt-out of appearing in the public ranking by enabling the **"Hide in Leaderboard"** option in your profile settings. Your contributions will still be counted in global statistics, but your name will be hidden.
*   **Account Deletion:** You can request the removal of your account and associated data by contacting the project administrator through the Web Portal or Telegram.

## 5. Third-Party Services
The extension relies on project infrastructure for authentication, database storage, and secure data delivery. These services are used only to provide the core YFull Finder functionality.
